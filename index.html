Meet Rob

Rob grew up in Mount Pleasant, SC and attended the College of Charleston where he majored in Accounting. After graduation, he worked in various operations roles before starting the Simply Lowcountry team with his wife Laura in 2014. After earning his real estate license, Rob assisted his wife in growing her business into a top producing real esate team. Rob learned the in’s and out’s of the real estate business during this time, as well as gained valuable experience in the selling and buying activities of luxury vacation resort communities.

In 2018, Laura grew her personal sales record by over 300%, placing her in the top 10% of Realtors in the Charleston Trident Association of Relators. At Simply Lowcountry, Rob has assisted numerous buyers and sellers to achieve their residential home buying goals. Rob took over the Simply Lowcountry team in 2020 after Laura entered the new homes market by joining Pulte group.

Rob is familiar with all the various neighborhoods, cities and towns of the Charleston area. Rob has assisted clients to buy and sell properties in Mount Pleasant, West Ashley, Summerville, John’s Island, James Island, Isle of Palms, Wild Dunes, Edisto Island and so many other communities.

The one thing that is most important to Rob is his client relationships. 98% of the business at Simply Lowcountry comes from past clients and referrals and we promise that it will show!
