Meet Laura

Laura is not new to Charleston. Having grown up in Mount Pleasant, SC, Laura attended the College of Charleston where she majored in Communication Studies. After graduating from The College, she obtained her South Carolina Real Estate License.

After receiving her real estate license, Laura worked as a Licensed Sales Associate to a top producing real estate executive at Kiawah Island Real Estate with a sales volume of over $20 million annually. Laura learned the in’s and out’s of the real estate business during this time, as well as gained valuable experience in the selling and buying activities of luxury vacation resort communities. In 2014, Laura joined the general MLS brokerage world, and created the brand “Simply Lowcountry”. At Simply Lowcountry, Laura has helped numerous buyers and sellers to achieve their home buying goals. In 2018, Laura grew her personal sales record by over 300%, placing her in the top 10% of Realtors in the Charleston Trident Association of Relators.

Laura is familiar with all the various neighborhoods, cities and towns of the Charleston area. Laura has helped clients to buy and sell properties in Mount Pleasant, West Ashley, Summerville, John’s Island, James Island, Isle of Palms, Wild Dunes, Edisto Island and so many other communities.

The one thing that is most important to Laura is her client relationships. 98% of her business comes from past clients and referrals and we promise that it will show! Averaging a 5/5 star rating on Zillow.com.
