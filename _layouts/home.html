Meet Rob

Rob grew up in Mount Pleasant, SC and attended the College of Charleston where he majored in Accounting. After graduation, Rob worked in various operations roles before helping to start the Simply Lowcountry team with his wife Laura in 2014.

In 2018, Laura achieved the Realtor of Distinction designation ranking her in the top 10% of Realtors in the Charleston Trident Association of Relators in terms of sales.
